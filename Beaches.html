Beautiful Beaches In Sri Lanka

Unawatuna Beach- For Tranquil Blue Waters

Unawatuna is a popular beach in Sri Lanka, famed for its calm blue waves. The prospect of witnessing a whale shark if you're lucky is the beach's unique selling factor. Because the rainy season ends in mid-October, the best time to visit is late August and early September.

  • Beach Is Famous For:
    • To enjoy water sports(snorkeling,scuba diving etc)
    • Easy travel to temples yatagala and maha viharaya
    • avalailability of massages and spa services
    • Turtle nesting

Weligama beach

Weligama Beach, on Sri Lanka's southwest coast, is one of the country's most popular beach resorts. It is believed to be Sri Lanka's most beautiful beach. Here you can take selfies with your family and friends. Make sure to post those photographs to Instagram so that everyone may view them. Best time to visit the beach: October to Aprill

  • Beach Is Famous For:
    • Water sports(snorkeling,scuba diving etc)
    • stilt fishing
    • avalailability of massages and spa services
    • Instagramable beaches
Hotels near Weligama beach

Hikkaduwa Beach

Some of the world's most lavish hotels and nightclubs may be found on Hikkaduwa Beach. This beach draws visitors from all across the world. Its promised natural beauty that hasn't been tampered , as well as physical and emotional relaxation, has been the reason for the hikkaduwa beach to be the best beaches in sri lanka. This beach is one of the top Sri Lanka beaches if you're not on a budget.

  • Beach Is Famous For:
    • Boat Rides
    • Hikkaduwa Beach festival
    • Coral sanctuary
